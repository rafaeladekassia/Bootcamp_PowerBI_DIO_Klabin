
# Hands-on Project: Xbox Sales Dashboard with Excel
## Project Description

The project aims to create a dashboard to display answers to specific business questions on sales performance of Xbox products: Game Pass, EA Pass, and Minecraft Pass.  The dashboard was created according to the following steps:

* insert an Assets tab in the worksheet to insert color palettes, images, and other visual identity resources;
* insert a tab with sales Database;
* insert a Calculation tab to answer specific business questions;
* create the Dashboard tab to organize calculated data to provide business insights clearly and aesthetically.

> *All data used here is fictional and for educational purpose.*

## Business Questions

The following business questions were considered in this project:
* What is the Total sales volume (R$) for the EA Play subscription plan?
* What is the Total sale volume (R$) for the Minecraft Season Pass subscription plan?

## Data modelling
The dashboard makes use of the following main entities:

* **Annual / Monthly / Quarterly filter:** Sidebar with filtering options to customize data visualization across different time intervals.
* **EA Total Sales value:** The total volume for the EA subscription in BRL (R$).
* **Minecraft Season Pass Total Sales value:** The total volume for the Minecraft Season Pass subscription in BRL (R$).
* **Graph view:** Visual comparison of sales volumes accross different time intervals (Yes: subscribed vs No: not subscribed).
